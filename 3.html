Vocabulary: Common Items

Let's learn some common items you might buy at a shop. Below are some examples:

Notebook - A book used for writing notes.

Pen - A tool for writing with ink.

Pencil - A tool for writing or drawing, usually with graphite inside.

Eraser - A tool used to remove pencil marks.

Water Bottle - A container used to carry water.

Snack - A small amount of food, usually eaten between meals.

Toy - An object used by children for play.
