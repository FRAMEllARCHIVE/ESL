Vocabulary: People & Verbs

Let's learn some important people and verbs related to shopping!

Shopkeeper - The person who works in a shop and sells things to customers.

Customer - A person who buys things from a shop.

Buy - To get something in exchange for money. Example: "I want to buy a notebook."

Pay - To give money for something you want to buy. Example: "I will pay with cash."

Look - To search or explore items in a shop. Example: "I want to look for a pencil."

Find - To locate something in the shop. Example: "I found my favorite snack!"

Great! Now you know some people and verbs you will use when shopping. Let's move on to learning some important phrases for shopping.
