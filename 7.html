Review & Wrap-Up

Let’s review what you’ve learned and wrap up today’s lesson!

1. Vocabulary Recap:

Items you can buy: notebook, pen, pencil, eraser, water bottle, snack, toy

Prices: dollar, cent

People in the shop: shopkeeper, customer

Verbs: buy, pay, look, find

2. Key Phrases Review:

"How much is this?"

"Can I help you?"

"I would like to buy..."

"Where can I find...?"

"Do you have...?"

"I’ll take it!"

3. Practice Recap:

You practiced using these phrases in a role-playing activity, where you took turns being the shopkeeper and the customer. Well done!

Great job today! You've learned important vocabulary and phrases for shopping, and you’ve practiced them through role-playing. Keep practicing, and you’ll feel more confident the next time you go shopping!
