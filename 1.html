Lesson: Buying Things at the Shop

Welcome to today's lesson! Today, we are going to learn how to buy things at a shop in English. You'll learn vocabulary, useful phrases, and how to have a conversation with a shopkeeper. By the end of this lesson, you will be able to talk about the items you want to buy, ask for prices, and use important shopping expressions in English.

Let's get started with a fun warm-up activity to begin thinking about things we like to buy!
