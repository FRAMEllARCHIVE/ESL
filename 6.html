Role-Playing Activity

Now, let’s practice what we’ve learned with a role-playing activity!

In this activity, you will play the role of either a shopkeeper or a customer.

Use the phrases and vocabulary you’ve learned to have a conversation in a shop. Here are the steps:

  1. Choose a partner or practice on your own.
  2. If you're the shopkeeper, greet the customer and ask if you can help.
  3. If you're the customer, ask about the price of an item or where to find something.
  4. Continue the conversation using the phrases you've learned (e.g., "How much is this?", "I would like to buy...", "Do you have...?").
  5. When the customer decides what to buy, use "I’ll take it!" to complete the conversation.

Example:

Shopkeeper: "Hello! Can I help you find something?"

Customer: "Yes, how much is this notebook?"

Shopkeeper: "The notebook is $2."

Customer: "I’ll take it!"

Now it's your turn! Have fun practicing!
