Shopping Vocabulary with Chinese Translations

Here are some shopping-related words and their Chinese translations!

Eraser: 橡皮 (Xiàngpí)
Water Bottle: 水瓶 (Shuǐpíng)
Snack: 小吃 (Xiǎochī)
Toy: 玩具 (Wánjù)
Dollar: 美元 (Měiyuán)
Cent: 分 (Fēn)
Shopkeeper: 店员 (Diànyuán)
Customer: 顾客 (Gùkè)
Buy: 买 (Mǎi)
Pay: 付钱 (Fùqián)
Look: 看 (Kàn)
Find: 找 (Zhǎo)
